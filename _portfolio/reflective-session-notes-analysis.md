## Project Overview
Processed and cleaned a dataset of 18,911 session notes with NLTK and TextBlob to analyze content structures and themes. Developed an expanded lexicons based on LIWC for growth mindset and knowledge acquisition to label and analyze the content of session notes to reflect the students' growth from tutoring sessions according to mentors' noting.

## Key Achievements
* **Large-Scale Analysis**: Processed 18,911 session notes
* **Advanced NLP**: Utilized NLTK and TextBlob for comprehensive text analysis
* **Custom Lexicon Development**: Expanded LIWC-based lexicons for specific educational contexts
* **Growth Mindset Analysis**: Developed metrics to assess student growth and knowledge acquisition
* **Mentorship Quality Assessment**: Created framework for evaluating tutoring effectiveness

## Impact
This research contributes to understanding how mentorship quality can be assessed through computational analysis of session notes, providing valuable insights for improving educational support systems and tutoring programs.
