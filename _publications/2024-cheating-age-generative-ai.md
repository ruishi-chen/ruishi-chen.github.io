**Status:** Work in Progress (September 2024 – Present)  
**Faculty Advisor:** Professor Victor R. Lee  
**Location:** Stanford, CA

This research project designs and tests survey items to capture respondents' immediate reactions to AI-powered technology. The study builds data pipelines in R to analyze responses and present results through interactive visualizations, examining how students navigate academic integrity in the age of generative AI.
